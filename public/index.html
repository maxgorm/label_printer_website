Frequently asked questions

Is this a pre-order?

Yes. Sentimo is currently available for pre-order. Shipping is expected to begin in August 2026.

When will orders ship?

We currently expect shipping to begin in August 2026. We will keep you updated via email if timelines change.

What happens if timelines change?

If manufacturing or logistics timelines change, we will email customers with updates. If we cannot fulfill your order, you will receive a full refund.

What comes in the box?

Each Sentimo set includes two printers, starter paper, charging cables, and quick setup materials so you can begin using them together right away.

Does it work with both iOS and Android?

Yes! The Sentimo app will be available on both the App Store and Google Play Store. You can pair devices across different operating systems seamlessly.

Do I need ink?

Never! Sentimo uses thermal printing technology, meaning no ink, ever. You just need to refill the paper rolls when they run out.

How long does the paper last?

One roll of thermal paper can print about 50-70 average-sized notes. The print itself lasts for years without fading if kept away from direct heat sources.

Can I print photos?

Absolutely. The app converts any photo from your gallery into a high-contrast black and white dithered image optimized for thermal printing.

Do I pay now or later?

Customers complete payment at checkout to reserve a unit from the first production run.

Can I cancel before it ships?

Yes. Pre-orders are fully refundable any time before shipment.

Is there a monthly subscription?

The core features of sending and receiving notes are completely free forever. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the essentials will always be free.
